# Onboarding Guide for Clients – Cassiopeia Intelligence

This document provides a **professional, detailed onboarding guide** for new clients implementing RAG-powered AI and enterprise solutions.

---

## 1. Consultation Process

* **Initial Meeting:**

  * Discuss client goals, challenges, and business objectives
  * Identify high-priority use cases and desired outcomes
* **Technical Assessment:**

  * Review existing systems, databases, and workflows
  * Assess data quality and availability for AI and RAG integration
* **Proposal & Strategy:**

  * Define scope, timeline, pricing, and technical approach
  * Outline RAG integration points and architecture recommendations

**Outcome:** Clear understanding of client needs and tailored solution roadmap.

---

## 2. Required Information & Documents

* **Business & Operational Data:** User interaction logs, customer queries, product metadata, or internal documents
* **Access Credentials:** API keys, database connections, SaaS or CRM access
* **Compliance & Regulatory Documents:** Data privacy policies, GDPR or other relevant compliance certifications
* **Branding & Design Assets:** Logos, style guides, color schemes, and UI requirements
* **Technical Specifications:** System architecture diagrams, workflows, and integration endpoints

**Purpose:** Ensures accurate, secure, and compliant setup of AI and RAG systems.

---

## 3. Project Timeline

* **Phase 1: Discovery & Requirements** – 1–2 weeks
* **Phase 2: Design & Architecture** – 2–3 weeks
* **Phase 3: Development & Integration** – 4–10 weeks (depending on solution complexity)
* **Phase 4: Testing & QA** – 1–2 weeks
* **Phase 5: Deployment & Training** – 1 week
* **Phase 6: Post-Deployment Monitoring & Optimization** – ongoing

**Note:** Timelines may vary based on project scope, RAG integration complexity, and data readiness.

---

## 4. Deliverables

* **Documentation:** System architecture, RAG pipeline design, and data schemas
* **Functional Solution:** Production-ready chatbot, recommendation system, or full-stack application
* **Integration:** Seamless connection with existing backend, frontend, and AI components
* **Training & Handover:** User guides, admin manuals, and team training sessions
* **Monitoring & Analytics Dashboards:** Real-time performance metrics, usage statistics, and RAG effectiveness

**Outcome:** Clients receive a complete, operational solution aligned with business objectives.

---

## 5. Support Channels

* **Dedicated Account Manager:** Single point of contact for project-related inquiries
* **Technical Support:** 24/7 support via email, chat, and ticketing systems
* **Documentation & Knowledge Base:** Access to guides, FAQs, and best practices
* **Training Sessions:** Optional onboarding workshops for client teams
* **Continuous Improvement:** Feedback loop for RAG model updates and feature enhancements

**Outcome:** Ensures clients have ongoing assistance, guidance, and support to maximize the value of their RAG-powered AI solutions.
