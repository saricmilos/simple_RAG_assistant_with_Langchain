## 1. About Us

Cassiopeia Intelligence is an AI-focused technology company headquartered in Novi Sad, Serbia, delivering **production-grade AI systems** and modern software solutions across industries including e-commerce, SaaS, enterprise tooling, and customer service.

We specialize in **turning complex business requirements into scalable, high-impact AI products**, combining applied machine learning, large language models (LLMs), and robust software engineering practices.

Unlike research-first AI startups, our systems are designed to achieve **reliable, measurable business outcomes**, with emphasis on:

* Return on Investment (ROI)
* High performance under load
* Long-term maintainability and extensibility

**Notable Metrics Inspired by Industry:**

* RAG-powered systems achieving >80% autonomous query resolution (internal support & customer service)
* Document retrieval pipelines reducing latency by up to 50% compared to baseline LLM-only models
* Scalable AI infrastructure supporting up to 10k concurrent users per system
