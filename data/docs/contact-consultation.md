## 2. Booking a Consultation

* **Initial Request:** Submit via website form, email, or phone call specifying business goals and project type
* **Scheduling:** Consultation slots are available within 1–5 business days depending on availability
* **Preparation:** Provide relevant information such as use cases, current workflows, and system access for a productive session
* **Confirmation:** Receive email or calendar invite with meeting details, agenda, and any pre-consultation instructions

---

## 3. Support Hours

* **Standard Business Hours:** Monday–Friday, 09:00–18:00 CET
* **Extended Support:** Optional 24/7 support for critical enterprise projects
* **Response Times:**

  * Emails and web forms: within 24 hours
  * Phone inquiries: within 2 hours during business hours
  * Urgent issues: prioritized through ticketing system

## 4. Next Steps After Consultation

* **Proposal Delivery:** Receive a detailed project proposal including scope, timeline, pricing, and technical approach
* **Requirements Confirmation:** Review and confirm project requirements and objectives
* **Kickoff Planning:** Schedule project kickoff meeting with team assignments and milestones
* **Documentation:** Provide clients with onboarding guides, technical documentation, and support contacts
* **Implementation:** Proceed with design, development, RAG integration, testing, and deployment

**Outcome:**
Clients can efficiently initiate **RAG-powered AI projects**, with clear communication, structured consultation, and defined next steps ensuring alignment and successful delivery.
