## 1. Pricing

* **Factors Influencing Pricing:**

  * Project scope and complexity
  * Number of features and modules (e.g., chatbots, recommendation engines, RAG integration)
  * Data processing and AI model training requirements
  * Deployment scale (cloud, on-premise, hybrid)
  * Ongoing support and maintenance level
* **Pricing Models:**

  * Fixed-price engagement for well-defined projects
  * Time & materials for flexible, iterative development
  * Subscription-based pricing for hosted AI solutions or SaaS products
* **Enterprise Benchmark:** Custom RAG-powered solutions typically range from $50k–$500k depending on scope and complexity.
